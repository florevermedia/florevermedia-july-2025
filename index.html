About Florevermedia

Florevermedia is a web design and development studio led by Manuela Flores — a Bolivian creative with a background in marketing agencies and social media strategy. Every website we build is tailored to the client’s needs, combining thoughtful design, technical precision, and SEO best practices.

What sets Florevermedia apart is our commitment to accessibility and ease of use. We design and personalize each website so that our clients can update it confidently — without worrying about breaking anything. And because we work in both English and Spanish, our websites help businesses connect with their audience — without language barriers.

Around the Web

Made with ❤️

Cami Caballero

Carolina Caballero is a personal image consultant and stylist based in Bolivia, offering services like wardrobe assessments and personal shopping. She also manages an online clothing boutique. Florevermedia designed and developed a WooCommerce website tailored to her brand’s elegance and authenticity. The final result was a sleek, user-friendly storefront in Spanish — making it easy for Carolina to promote both her services and products. After launch, she saw increased client engagement and a noticeable boost in sales.

Portafolio Creativo

Portafolio Creativo is a group of Bolivian architecture students who launched a small online academy to teach software skills to fellow architecture students. Florevermedia worked closely with them to create a professional, modern website that showcases their courses, facilitates communication with prospective students, and positions their brand as both educational and creative. The platform was designed to be scalable and intuitive, allowing the team to easily update their offerings and manage their own content as they grow.

Jewelry Ro

Jewelry Ro is a Colombian family jewelry brand co-founded by Valeria and her family. After moving to the U.S., Valeria partnered with her father — a master jeweler based in Colombia — and her siblings to bring handcrafted Colombian pieces to a wider audience. Florevermedia designed and developed a bilingual e-commerce website that honors the brand’s heritage while making it easy for customers in both Colombia and the United States to explore and shop. The final site combines storytelling, elegance, and functionality to help Jewelry Ro grow internationally while keeping its roots at the center.
